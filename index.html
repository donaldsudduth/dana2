Dana Lee Capanna

(she/her)



Actor | Mover| Dancer | Choreographer


South Philadelphia based theatre artist with a passion for movement and choreography. She believes that everybody deserves equal opportunity to let their voice be heard, and if someone communicates well through their body, with dance and movement, then she would like to encourage them, assist them, teach them, and uplift them while on that journey. She is also on her own journey of exploring movement that can reflect her beliefs/stories, then translate them into a teachable dance that she can articulate well and inspire others with, and then let go of control and allow for people to interpret the dance in their own beautiful, unique way. She feels this type of approach to dance and movement is the best way to highlight the important belief that all people, all bodies, no matter what size, color, shape, or age should dance if they feel moved to move. Because the beauty of dance is not just in the aesthetic, but in the joy that it allows us all to share.
